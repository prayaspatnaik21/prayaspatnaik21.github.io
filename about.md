# About Prayas Patnaik

I'm a Perception Engineer with a passion for building robust imaging systems and optimizing visual quality in real-time applications.

## Background

With an MS in Computer Engineering from the University of Texas at Dallas and a BTech in Electronics & Communication from NIT Surat, I've built a strong foundation in both software and hardware engineering.

My career has spanned from **large-scale infrastructure** at John Deere to **precision engineering** at RFA Engineering, where I've worked on mission-critical systems for agricultural autonomy and automotive perception.
